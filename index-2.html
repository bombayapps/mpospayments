Mintoak Merchant Pay

Multiple payment modes, disparate reports mean a reconciliation nightmare!! With Mintoak accepting all types of digital payments is super easy. The Mintoak transaction app is super cool with a range of features designed to make your life simpler,. sSo that you can focus on growing your business.

  • Cards : You can accept Credit cards, Debit Cards and Prepaid cards.

  • UPI-BharatQR : Scan & Pay – Your customer can pay with any of the supported bank apps, Google Pay, Phonepe, BHIM, WhatsApp and more.
